BotFather guide (2 minutes)
  1. Open Telegram and go to @BotFather.
  2. Tap Start, then send /newbot.
  3. Pick a name for your coach (anything you like).
  4. Pick a username ending in bot (example: mycoach_bot).
  5. BotFather will send a token like 123456:ABC-DEF.... Copy it.

What’s a bot token? It’s a secret password that lets your coach bot run. Don’t share it publicly. If it leaks, you can regenerate it in BotFather with /token.
