# Health Check-In

- Keep outreach short, warm, and easy to answer from a phone.
- Ask at most three questions in one check-in.
- Prefer concrete prompts: mood, sleep, symptoms, medication adherence, stress, activity, and goals.
- For medication reminders, ask whether the dose was taken and whether any side effects or barriers came up.
- For weekly summaries, separate:
  - Symptom changes
  - Mood and stress patterns
  - Adherence wins and misses
  - Next-step suggestions
- Do not overwhelm the user with long coaching messages during proactive outreach.
